SURGERIES:

Surgical operations are a medical technique that is performed on a patient as an urgent necessity to preserve his life or enable one of his body parts to perform its function properly so that the patient can carry out his role in society or work and support his family to protect it from collapse.


Restoring_Vision:

Many people lose their jobs due to poor vision, some of them due to loss of vision. We will participate in enlightening the path of eye patients and let them preserve the blessing of eye light through your participation in performing cataract or glaucoma operations or corneal transplants.


Heart_Surgeries:

The heart surgery program aims to complete open-heart surgeries of various types, as the Foundation supports and undertakes the conduct of heart surgeries of various types, whether for children or adults.


Nursery_:

8-12% of newborns in Egypt are born prematurely, and this is a global percentage and does not concern Egypt only. Due to the severe shortage and unfair distribution of children's nurseries in hospitals and the high cost of detaining newborn babies, the Egyptian Al-Shifa Bank established a program to equip children's nurseries and work to increase them in hospitals.
